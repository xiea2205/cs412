About Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein (1879-1955) was a German-born theoretical physicist who is widely regarded as one of the greatest and most influential scientists of all time. He developed the theory of relativity, one of the two pillars of modern physics alongside quantum mechanics.

Key Achievements:

  • Developed the theory of special relativity (1905)
  • Formulated the mass-energy equivalence formula E=mcÂ²
  • Won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921
  • Developed the theory of general relativity (1915)
  • Made significant contributions to quantum theory

Fun Facts:

  • He was a passionate violin player
  • He had a famously messy hairstyle
  • He was known for his thought experiments
  • He was a strong advocate for civil rights and peace
